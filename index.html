About

Bluemania is the solo project of Nick Jenkins.

Between 2003 and 2009, Nick and Gareth Lewis spent their evenings in Melbourne, Australia making music on laptops — between exams, road trips, and late-night MSN conversations. The tracks were rough, weird, and never finished. They lived on hard drives and in chat logs for almost twenty years.

In 2026, those original cuts were reimagined and remastered with majority AI assistance using Suno. The track names, the album title, even the concept — all pulled from real chat archives. "We need to sit down and analyse these 'dinner with chris' tracks" was a real message from 2006.

This is what happens when you finally finish something you started twenty years ago.
